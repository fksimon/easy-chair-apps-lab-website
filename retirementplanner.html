Features

Tax-Aware Projections

Year-by-year projections covering federal income tax, capital gains, NIIT, Medicare IRMAA, ACA premium tax credits, and all 50 states plus D.C. Estate tax modeled at federal and state levels.

Social Security Optimizer

Evaluate claiming ages 62 to 70 for yourself and your spouse. Breakeven analysis, COLA projections, and the long-term dollar impact of each strategy.

Roth Conversion Engine

Bracket-targeting and IRMAA-aware Roth conversion recommendations. Supports minimize-tax and target-amount modes across a multi-year planning horizon.

Monte Carlo Risk Analysis

Historical simulation using 97 years of actual S&P 500 returns (1928–2024) plus configurable parametric simulation. Reports success rate, median ending balance, and sequence-of-returns risk.

6-Tier Withdrawal Strategy

Six configurable withdrawal tiers across eight named strategies — tax-bracket-fill, proportional, Roth-last, and more. Dynamic guardrails set a ceiling and floor on year-to-year spending.

Scenario Comparison & Sensitivity

Side-by-side comparison of two plans. Tornado-chart sensitivity analysis shows which assumptions — return, inflation, withdrawal, Social Security timing — most affect your outcome.

Why Retirement Planner?

1

On-device calculations. No backend, no subscription.

Every projection runs on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac. Nothing is sent to a server. No account required. No recurring fee.

2

Comprehensive tax modeling

Federal brackets, all 50 states plus D.C., capital gains, Net Investment Income Tax, Medicare IRMAA surcharges, and ACA premium tax credits — all in a single integrated engine.

3

Optional iCloud sync through your own Apple account

Enable iCloud and your plans sync privately across your devices. Easy Chair Apps Lab never has access to your data — it stays in your iCloud account.

4

97 years of real market data

Monte Carlo historical simulation uses actual S&P 500 annual returns from 1928 to 2024, not synthetic distributions. Parametric mode lets you configure your own assumptions.

5

Native on iPhone, iPad, and Mac

Built with SwiftUI and Mac Catalyst for native rendering on all three platforms. The same plan files and calculations, wherever you are.

No account. No analytics. No network calls.

Your financial data lives on your device. Retirement Planner has no analytics SDK, no telemetry, and no sign-in. The only Apple-managed network path is the App Store for the Pro purchase. The privacy manifest confirms zero data collection.
