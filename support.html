Support

We're here to help.

Thank you for using Easy Chair Apps Lab apps. If you have a question, run into a problem, or just want to share feedback, we'd love to hear from you.

Contact Us

The best way to reach us is by email. We read every message and do our best to respond promptly.

support@easychairappslab.com

What to Include

To help us assist you as quickly as possible, please include:

  • The name of the app you're using
  • A brief description of the issue or question
  • Your device model and iOS version (found in Settings → General → About)
  • Any steps that reliably reproduce the issue, if applicable

App Store Reviews

If you enjoy an app, leaving a review on the App Store is a wonderful way to support the work. If something isn't working as expected, please email us first — we'd much rather fix the problem than have you leave frustrated.

Privacy

Any information you share with us by email is used only to respond to your inquiry. We do not store it, share it, or use it for marketing. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
