Features

Monthly Calendar

Tap any day to plan a shift — set hours, start time, and optional notes. Your whole month at a glance.

Bulk Entry & Sync

Add multiple planned days in one flow; the editor advances date automatically. Planned shifts sync to your iPhone Calendar with a customisable event title.

One-Tap Logging

Tap a day to record actual hours. The editor shows your plan alongside so you can spot the difference at a glance.

Time-Off Tagging

Mark days as PTO, Sick, or Holiday from a compact dropdown. Time-off hours are tracked separately and included in per-period totals.

Per-Period Totals

Regular hours, overtime tier 2, overtime tier 3, and PTO computed automatically for every pay period — no manual math required.

Reconcile Periods

Mark a period as reconciled once your payslip confirms it. Reconciled periods get a visual badge and move off your unresolved list.

Email Reports

Send a clean hours summary directly from the app. The email template is fully editable with a token system — mix and match period dates, hour totals, and a day-by-day breakdown.

Five Overtime Modes

Daily threshold, two-tier daily, weekly, and plan-relative modes cover the most common overtime structures. Configure once and the math takes care of itself.

Year-to-Date Reports

Monthly summaries with day-by-day breakdowns, year-to-date running totals, upcoming schedule by period, and a quick list of unreconciled periods.

Why Hourly Time Tracker?

1

Built for your overtime rules, not generic ones

Five modes cover the most common overtime structures: daily threshold, two-tier daily, weekly, or plan-relative. Configure once and forget.

2

Plan and log in the same calendar

Most time trackers are log-only. Hourly Time Tracker shows planned and actual side by side so you can spot the gap instantly.

3

Private by design

No analytics SDK, no crash reporting, no advertising identifiers. All data lives in your private iCloud account — not on any server.

4

Clean email reports, your way

The email template is fully editable with a token system — swap in any combination of period dates, hour totals, and a day-by-day breakdown.

5

iCloud sync with no setup

Works on every iPhone signed into the same Apple ID. Nothing to configure — your hours just appear on every device.

No account. No ads. No tracking.

Your hours stay in your iCloud account. Hourly Time Tracker has no analytics, no crash reporting, and no advertising identifiers. What you work is your business.
