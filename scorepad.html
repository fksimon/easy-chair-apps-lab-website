Features

Two Scoring Modes

Round-by-round grid entry for structured games like Catan or Hearts, or quick +/- steppers for running totals like darts or bowling.

Smart Auto-Suggest

Start typing a game or player name and ScorePad fills in past records instantly. No retyping regulars.

Full History

Every session is saved automatically. Tap any past game to review the complete scoresheet with all rounds.

Stats

Games-per-month charts, top games and players by play count, and a most-recent sessions list — all without leaving the app.

Random Start Picker

Everyone places a finger on screen. After a countdown, one finger lights up at random. No arguments about who goes first.

Player Profiles

Persistent player profiles with full play history. The same name resolves to the same player every time.

Why ScorePad?

1

Minimal time away from the table

The setup form is one screen; scoring is one more. No onboarding, no sign-in, no tutorial popups.

2

Two modes, one app

Most scorekeeping apps pick one style. ScorePad handles both round-based grids and running +/- totals.

3

Remembers everything, asks nothing

Game names, player names, round counts — all reused automatically on the next play.

4

Completely private

No analytics SDK, no network requests, no account. All data lives on-device with SwiftData. The privacy manifest confirms zero data collection.

5

Any game, any group

Card games, board games, dice games, darts, bowling, mini-golf — anything with players and scores.

No account. No ads. No tracking.

Your scores stay on your device. ScorePad has no network requests, no analytics, and no sign-in. What happens at game night stays at game night.

ScorePad is free. If it saves your game night, a small tip keeps it going.
